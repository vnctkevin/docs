# Reports

Ellipsis can crawl your commits, summarize the changes, and create full reports. The style of the report depends on the task at hand, choose from:

*   Summaries of important changes
*   Release note creations
*   High level digests
*   and more...

The reports will be delivered via email or Slack.

Each report pertains to a specified repositores over a time range spanning 1 day to 1 year.
